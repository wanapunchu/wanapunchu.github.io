This page houses (some) of my research background. I'm eternally grateful to all my mentors and colleagues.

Math

Since February 2024, I've been working with Dr. Giada Franz to study free boundary minimal surfaces in the unit ball. A preprint of our paper is available on here.

In summer 2024, I worked with Christine Gao and Ananya Prasanna under Professor Alex Wright and Dr. George Domat at the University of Michigan REU. We studied geometric group theory, with particular interest in nonhyperbolic graphs with Out(Fn) actions. Fun fact: if you go onto the REU seminar website, you can see me with blond hair (unusual) standing in a river (also unusual).

Materials Science

I spent the summer of 2023 programming a Python-based hardware-software interface for a position-sensitive neutron detector at the MIT Nuclear Reactor Laboratory supervised by Dr. Boris Khaykovich and Dr. Sean Fayfar. You can find the GitHub repository for the project here.

Astronomy

In high school, I researched the magnetic activity of low-mass stars in Professor Marcel Agüeros' group at Columbia University under the supervision of Dr. Alejandro Núñez. This work resulted in an AAS conference proceeding and was incorporated in a paper in the ApJ.
