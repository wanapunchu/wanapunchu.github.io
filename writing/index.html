On campus, I'm associate news editor at The Tech (MIT's student newspaper), where I also occasionally write about music or campus life. For the past two years, I've written for Infinite (the design magazine). I'm also on the editorial team at Rune (the literary/arts magazine), although I don't write my own pieces there. In my free time, I write poetry and fiction. WIPs and thoughts on "craft" come out approximately monthly on my Substack. Occasionally, I dormspam polls and write up the results.
