On campus, I'm associate news editor at The Tech (MIT's student newspaper) and a contributor to Infinite (the design magazine). I'm also on the editorial team at Rune (the literary/arts magazine), although I don't write my own pieces there. Occasionally, I dormspam polls and write up the results, e.g. Love@MIT from 2024 and 2025.

In my free time, I mainly write poetry, but I've also dabbled in fiction and CNF. Embarrassing WIPs and thoughts on "craft" come out approximately monthly on my (private) Substack. If I don't approve your subscription request, feel free to reach out.
