About

Hi! My name is Sabine (she/her). I'm a junior at MIT studying math and urban planning. Outside class, I like to read, run, listen to music, and bake. Right now, I'm thinking a lot about...

  • Constrained verse forms, what fiction ought to represent, making writing breathe
  • Environmental justice, public transit, effective community-building, sidewalks
  • Innovation without the jargon, enabling more equitable access to capital, the roles of philanthropy and advocacy

I blog on my Substack, which includes short fiction, informal life updates, and longer personal essays. Also, Infinite Magazine only comes out once a year, but I'm proud of the writing, modeling, and design I've done there. There's a highlights page on this website for my best work.
